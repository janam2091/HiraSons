About Hira Sons


Founded over five decades ago, we stand firm on the pillars of integrity, trust, and unwavering commitment to excellence. Our journey is not merely a testament to our longevity, but a reflection of the enduring relationships we have cultivated with our clients, partners, and communities.

Our name "Hira" Sons is derived from the Sanskrit word "hīraka," meaning "diamond," literally translates to ‘Diamond Sons or Sons of Diamonds’ highlighting our passion and commitment to the brilliance and timelessness associated with this precious gemstone.

Why Us?


For over half a century, we have delivered exquisite gems and followed ethical & responsible practices to establish a responsible supply chain that elevates the diamond industry and brings value to our customers and stakeholders.

We take our Responsibilities Seriously


Our commitment to responsible jewelry practices includes ethical conduct, fair treatment of individuals, and environmental stewardship. We prioritize product integrity, supply chain transparency, and stakeholder engagement to uphold industry standards and promote sustainability. Join us in our journey towards a responsible and ethical jewelry industry.
